Fred the Baker Baking Company is a vibrant cafe famous for their lavish donuts. After countless years of operations, Fred the Baker is ready to retire and spend the rest of his life in his dream location of the Turk and Caicos Islands. However, although he may be leaving his love for donuts, his love for donuts never will leave him and he hopes to keep it surviving and thriving with this game where users like you can create your own virtual donuts for all to enjoy. He hopes you enjoy his game and sends his best regards from the beach.
